Executive Summary

The goal of this project is to create an annual data set of earnings, cash flows, and leverage ratios for all stocks. All data was taken from the CRSP/Compustat Merged-Fundamentals data set.

Calculations for the Annual Data Set

WRDS was used to query the datasets required for these calculations. The stock universe included all stocks CRSP has within their database for all years. The earnings was taken directly as earnings per share excluding extraordinary items. Cash flow was calculated by adding together financing activities net cash flow, investing activities net cash flow, and operating activities net cash flow. Financial debt to total assets was calculated by dividing longterm debt plus short term debt by total assets. leverage ratio based on market was calculated by taking long and short term debt and dividing by the market value. Leverage ratio based on book was calculated by dividing total debt by total equity.

new_data <- dset_data
new_data[, date := as.Date(as.character(datadate), format = "%Y%m%d") ]
new_data[, earnings_per_share := epspx]
new_data[, cashflow := fincf+ivncf+oancf]
new_data[, FDTA := (dltt+dlc)/at]
new_data[, lev_ratio_market := (dltt+dlc)/(mkvalt)]
new_data[, lev_ratio_book := (dt)/(teq)]

new_data[, year := year(date)]
# calculate earnings per share cashflow and various lev ratios sorted by year and gvkey
fundamental_data <- new_data[, 
              .(na.omit(earnings_per_share), (na.omit(cashflow)), na.omit(FDTA), na.omit(lev_ratio_market),na.omit(lev_ratio_book)),
              by = .(year, gvkey)]
colnames(fundamental_data) <- c('year','gvkey', 'earnings_per_share', 'cashflow','fdta','lev_ratio_market','lev_ratio_book')

save(fundamental_data, file="./Fundamentals.RData")
